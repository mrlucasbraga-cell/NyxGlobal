Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy governs the use of the mobile application "Security Cam" created for Android devices. The Application functions as a local security utility that allows users to stream video and audio between devices within the same local Wi-Fi network.

1. Information Collection and Use

The Application does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information, personal data, or user communications. All video, audio, and motion detection processing is performed locally and in real time on the user's device. No data is uploaded to external servers or cloud services.

2. Device Permissions

  • Camera: Required for real-time video monitoring.
  • Microphone: Required for audio streaming and talk/listen features.
  • Notifications: Required to maintain foreground services on Android 13+.

These permissions are used exclusively for local functionality and never for external data collection.

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The Application uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect pseudonymous identifiers such as Advertising ID to provide relevant ads and measure performance.

4. Children's Privacy

The Application is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Users are encouraged to review this page regularly.

6. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, please contact us by email.
