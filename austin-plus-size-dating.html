Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Austin Dating (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (Austin Dating). By using our application, you explicitly consent to the data practices described in this policy in accordance with Google Play Developer Policies.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide a better experience and match you with other users:

  • Account Information: When you register, you provide your name, email address, date of birth, gender, sexual orientation, and photos.
  • Sensitive Personal Data: With your explicit consent, we process profile details you choose to share, such as dating preferences, interests, and profile bios.
  • Precise Location Data: To connect you with nearby users, we collect your precise geographic location (GPS) while the app is in use. You can enable or disable location services at any time via your device settings.
  • Device and Usage Information: We collect IP addresses, device IDs, operating system versions, and app crash logs for performance optimization and security.

2. How We Use and Process Your Data

We process your data strictly to fulfill our services:

• To create and manage your user account.

• To display your profile to other users according to your match preferences.

• To facilitate communications and messaging between matched users.

• To improve app security, detect fraud, prevent harassment, and enforce our Terms of Service.

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We value your privacy and do not sell your personal data. Your data is shared only under the following circumstances:

• Public Profile: Your profile photos, name, age, and bio are visible to other users within the app.

• Service Providers: We may use trusted third-party analytics and hosting tools (such as Google Analytics for Firebase) that comply with strict data protection standards.

• Legal Obligations: We may disclose data if required by law or to protect the safety of our users.

4. Data Retention and Account Deletion Policy

We store your personal data only as long as your account remains active. You have the right to access, rectify, or delete your data at any time.

• You can request the permanent deletion of your account and all associated personal data directly within the app settings or by visiting our dedicated Account Deletion page: [INSERT_YOUR_DELETION_URL_HERE].

• Upon deletion, your data will be permanently wiped from our active databases within 48 hours, except where legal retention obligations apply.

5. Children’s Privacy (COPPA and GDPR Compliance)

Austin Dating is strictly intended for individuals who are 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal data from children under 18. If we discover that a minor has created an account, we will terminate the account and permanently delete all associated data immediately.

6. Security Measures

We implement rigorous industry-standard technical and organizational security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or alteration.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or require assistance regarding this Privacy Policy or your data rights, contact us.
